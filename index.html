Our Company

Brusa & Asociados Traductores / Translators was founded as a translation and language training services firm based on the pillar that good service
has been - and shall always be- our outstanding feature.

We are client-satisfaction driven and our dedication towards such aim is our main differentiator. What’s more, response capacity is our guaranteed added value which results from our being a network of professionals with a clear and compromised team work enabling us to take up bulky workloads and render services in a timely fashion even when deadlines are of a seemingly impossible nature.

As in any like firm, we foresee many challenges and we thank our clients for relying on us, which reality enables us to permanently consolidate and enhance our team work.

Even though we recognise ourselves as inhabitants of a global village, we consider that personal contact with our clientele is of the essence in the provision of a good service. That is the main reason why we do our best to get to know our clients personally. It is our clients to whom we commit our daily work.
